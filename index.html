NurseNote — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026  ·  Developer: Yoonified  ·  Contact: contact@yoonified.com

1. Overview

NurseNote is a mobile application designed for licensed healthcare professionals to log patient vitals during clinical shifts. This policy explains how data is handled within the app.

2. Data Collection

NurseNote does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • All data entered in the app (vitals, notes, patient identifiers) is stored locally on your device only.
  • No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry is collected.
  • No account registration is required.

3. Patient Data

NurseNote does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations. It is a logging tool only. The app is intended for use by licensed healthcare professionals in accordance with their institution's policies.

Because all data remains on-device, the user is solely responsible for compliance with applicable healthcare privacy regulations (such as HIPAA) within their institution.

4. Permissions

NurseNote requests only the permissions necessary to function. No camera, microphone, location, or contact access is requested or used.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All app data is stored locally and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app. There is no account or cloud backup to delete.

6. Children

NurseNote is intended for use by adult healthcare professionals only. It is not directed at children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change in a future version (for example, optional cloud sync), this policy will be updated before that version is released and users will be notified within the app.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email contact@yoonified.com.
